Email Marketing For SME's: Worth The Effort?

Over its 50 year history, email has grown into an incredibly powerful and cost-effective tool for direct marketing on a massive scale while providing marketers with a way to observe and react to consumer behaviour like never before.

Despite the abundance of tools available to brands looking to market themselves, email marketing is still one of the best bets for growth.
Over its 50 year history, email has grown into an incredibly powerful and cost-effective tool for direct marketing on a massive scale while providing marketers with a way to observe and react to consumer behaviour like never before.
As time progressed, more marketers hopped on the email marketing bandwagon leading to inboxes being cluttered with spam messages, this prompted privacy regulations to be strengthened and made it mandatory for consumers to be able to opt-out of unsolicited messages.
The ever-tightening regulations made marketers realize that they needed to engage consumers with their messaging by making it timely and relevant to avoid being blacklisted.

Email marketing has evolved over the years, and in 2021 its become less about simply sending a well-worded sales copy and more about creating an impactful experience that resonates with your prospects.
Here are a few ways for you to do just that.

5 Ways To Get The Most Out Of Email Marketing For Your Brand

  • Maintain authenticity above all else
    • There’s nothing a consumer values more than a brand that gives off an authentic identity and demonstrates a genuine commitment to its goals.
      Modern consumers aren’t easily swayed by catchy sales copy or vague promises, over 60% of consumers will arrive at a purchase decision only if a brand is in line with their beliefs.
  • Embrace interactive content
    • Interactive content enables your customers to experience your brand from an entirely new level.
      The inclusion of dynamic design features such as animated carousels, star ratings and questionnaires can boost your click-to-open rate by up to 73% with embedded videos boosting rates by up to 300%
  • Optimize for mobile
    • If your email or web page doesn’t work on mobile in 2021, it’s best if it doesn’t work at all. Enhance the user experience by:
      • Keeping your message concise
      • Keep subject lines short and on point
      • Make your CTA’s (call to action) prominent

      More than half of all emails sent are read on mobile, but almost half of them don’t have proper formatting, which is now an essential component of crafting the perfect email. Here’s an example of a finely crafted email from TripAdvisor.

  • Take advantage of trigger-based marketing
    • Automation is a lifesaver for marketers, you can leverage more control over your processes with trigger-based emails which are event-based marketing strategies that are launched by a certain trigger on your website eg- abandoning your online shopping cart or searching for relevant topics, this tactic is known to perform 3x better than nurture and batch emails
      It also helps track a customer/ prospects interaction with your email/ website and sends out appropriate responses to garner their attention. These can also consist of welcome emails, onboarding emails, transactional emails etc
  • Personalize
    • Establish familiarity and humanize your interactions, as emails with personalized subject lines are 26% more likely to be opened. Take note to not sound weird or pushy, as this may have the opposite effect.

So we now know that email marketing is still relevant and impactful today, if you still find yourself asking ‘why’ and ‘what’ when it comes to ease of use and costs involved, read on to find out more.
According to Forbes, the Return On Investment for social media is 95%.
Can you guess the ROI for email marketing? An eye-watering 4200%
So in terms of ROI, email marketing absolutely crushes social media.

The Benefits of Email Marketing For Small Businesses

Email marketing is a very effective strategy for small to medium enterprises (even large organizations) and helps grow and maintain your client base, here are a few more benefits of this proven strategy.

  • It’s cost-effective
    • Email marketing is one of the most cost-effective marketing strategies available, many great email service providers such as MailChimp and SendinBlue provide great value with their free packages and have attractive pricing if your needs surpass them.
  • Easy to use
    • Using an email service is dead-easy and its main components can be handled within one provider, the 4 pillars of email marketing are as follows
      • Capture email addresses
      • Manage campaigns and lists
      • Design email campaigns
      • Deliver email campaigns

      Once you’ve gone through this process and created a killer campaign, you can send your targeted message to all your prospects/ customers with the click of a button.

  • Quick to perform
    • Unlike social media, email marketing doesn’t need to be constantly monitored and looked after, it can be as and when you need. A monthly or weekly newsletter may be all you need to stay on your customer’s minds.
  • Better brand recognition and credibility
    • Increased credibility leads to fewer of your emails ending up ignored, deleted or in the ‘Spam’ folder. Having a well-recognized brand means that readers will look forward to your messages and potentially provide valuable feedback on your brand.
  • Increased web traffic
    • An email is a great way to drive some traffic to your growing website, a small email campaign is all it takes to get readers to engage with content on your site or blog.

The biggest advantage of email marketing is the level of control that it offers to any organization, you should pay close attention to the churn rate of your mailing lists, in other words, this is the ratio of people subscribing to your mailing list vs unsubscribers.
If you have a healthy list, you should be seeing more subscribers than unsubscribers, else it may become fatigued and stale if it’s not replenished with new prospects over time. Make sure to maintain a healthy list and segment them according to the information you’ve gathered about them such as age group, Profession, interests to target them better.

Wrap Up

Email remains an incredibly powerful channel that gives you an unparalleled level of accessibility. Personalized interactive content that engages readers coupled with mobile responsiveness is a winning combo for email marketing.
